common questions

a few things
worth knowing.

Is this actually AI? Who made it?
Yes — Hayley uses AI to analyse what you say and surface what's underneath. It was built by a founder obsessed with the gap between thinking something and understanding it. I made this because I kept circling the same thoughts, and nothing I tried actually helped me process — only replay. So I built something that listens differently.
Can anyone see my entries?
No. Transcription happens on your device — your voice audio never leaves your phone. Your entries are private by design, not by policy. We can't read them even if we wanted to.
What happens during onboarding?
When you first open Hayley, a short onboarding flow walks you through how it works — including a sample insight so you can see exactly what to expect. You'll make your first voice recording before reaching the paywall, so you know what you're signing up for before committing anything.
Can I read back my old entries?
Yes. Every entry is saved with its date, the three insight cards, and the pattern Hayley detected. You can scroll back through your history at any time. The point isn't to re-read your words — it's to watch the patterns emerge over time.
What happens after the 14-day trial?
Choose annual (€49.99/year, around 30% off) or monthly (€5.99/month). Both start with a 14-day free trial. Cancel anytime. If you don't continue after the trial, your entries stay on your device — we don't delete anything.
Is this a replacement for therapy?
No, and we'd never claim otherwise. Hayley is a thinking companion — it helps you process and understand your own thoughts more clearly. It has no therapeutic intent, no clinical framework, and no agenda. If you're experiencing significant distress, please speak to a professional. Hayley works best alongside a clear head, not instead of support.
