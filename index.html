how it works

01
speak for sixty seconds

No prompts. No structure. Just tap and talk. Say whatever is on your mind — unfiltered, unedited. Hayley transcribes in real time as you speak.

02
receive a real reflection

Not a summary. Not a restatement. Hayley surfaces the thing you were circling — names the emotional state, identifies the pattern, asks the question you hadn't thought to ask.

03
patterns emerge over time

Each entry builds context. After a few sessions, hayley starts noticing what you consistently avoid, what language you reach for under pressure, what keeps coming back.

04
one step, each week

A weekly reflection surfaces what slowed you down, what you sidestepped, and — without judgement — one concrete thing to try differently.
