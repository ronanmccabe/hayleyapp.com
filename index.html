common questions

a few things worth knowing.

Is this actually AI? Who made it?
Yes — hayley uses AI to analyse what you say and surface what's underneath. It was built by a small team obsessed with the gap between thinking something and understanding it. We made this because we kept circling the same thoughts ourselves, and nothing we tried actually helped us process — only replay. So we built something that listens differently.
Can anyone see my entries?
No. Transcription happens on your device — your voice never leaves your phone. Your entries are private by design, not by policy. We can't read them even if we wanted to.
Can I read back my old entries?
Yes. Every entry is saved with its date, the three insight cards, and the pattern hayley detected. You can scroll back through your history at any time. The point isn't to re-read your words — it's to watch the patterns emerge over time.
What happens after the 7-day trial?
If you want to keep going, it's €5.99/month — no commitment, cancel in one tap. If you don't continue, your entries stay on your device. We don't delete anything. No card is required to start the trial.
Is this a replacement for therapy?
No, and we'd never claim otherwise. Hayley is a thinking companion — it helps you process and understand your own thoughts more clearly. It has no therapeutic intent, no clinical framework, and no agenda. If you're experiencing significant distress, please speak to a professional. Hayley works best alongside a clear head, not instead of support.
